Monty's Signature Burritos

"When you get somthing right, the onus is on you to do anything different"

-Monty Dillion

Ingredients

  • 2 Uncooked Tortilla Land tortillas
  • 3 Morning Star veggie sausages
  • 1/2 Can black beans
  • 1 Cup of grape tomatoes, diced
  • Sour cream to taste
  • El Yucateco hot sauce to taste
  • 1 Handfull Tillamook sharp shredded cheddar

Instructions

  1. Heat a large flat pan on low for several minutes
  2. While the pan is heating, dice the tomatoes
  3. Start cooking the first tortilla
  4. Microwave the vegitarian sausages for 90 seconds
  5. Flip the tortilla, add half the cheese to melt on top
  6. Remove sausage from microwave, in a small bowl combine with beans
  7. Remove first totilla and begin cooking the second
  8. Microwave the sausage/bean mixture for 90 seconds
  9. Flip the second tortilla, adding the remainder of the cheese to melt on top
  10. Remove the second tortilla from pan
  11. Add sausage/bean mixture evenly to each tortilla
  12. Add the tomatoes evenly to each tortilla
  13. Add a dollop of sour cream to each
  14. Add hot sauce to taste
  15. Wrap tortillas into burritos and serve
